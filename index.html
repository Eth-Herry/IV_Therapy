Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Is IV therapy safe?
Yes. All our treatments are physician-approved and administered by licensed registered nurses. We conduct a health intake screening before every session, and all ingredients are pharmacy-grade sterile compounds.
How long does a session take?
Most sessions run 30–60 minutes depending on the drip. NAD+ sessions are longer at 2–4 hours due to the infusion rate requirements. You're welcome to relax, use your phone, or simply rest during your session.
Will I feel it during the infusion?
Most clients feel a mild coolness at the insertion site and sometimes a slight vitamin taste. It's generally very comfortable. Our nurses are experts at ensuring minimal discomfort and will monitor you throughout.
How often should I come?
It depends on your goals. Many clients come once or twice per month for general wellness. Athletes or those recovering from illness may benefit from more frequent sessions. Our medical team will help you build a personalised plan.
Do you offer mobile or at-home service?
Absolutely. Our mobile service brings the full VIVA Drip experience to your home, hotel, or private event. Mobile bookings are available with 24-hour advance notice. A travel fee may apply depending on distance.
