Preparing for Your Visit

What to expect on the day

A few things to know before your VIVA Drip session so you can arrive relaxed and ready.

01
Arrive Well-Hydrated

Drink at least 16 oz of water before your session. Good baseline hydration makes it easier to find a vein and maximises your results.

02
Eat a Light Meal

Have a light snack or meal 30–60 minutes beforehand. Arriving on an empty stomach can occasionally cause light-headedness during high-dose vitamin infusions.

03
Relax & Enjoy

Wear comfortable clothing with short or loose sleeves for easy IV access. Bring headphones, a book, or just enjoy the peaceful lounge environment.
